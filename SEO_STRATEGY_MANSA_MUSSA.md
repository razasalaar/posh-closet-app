## Technical SEO Priorities

- Improve Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS)
- Ensure mobile-first layout and CTA visibility
- Convert/compress images to WebP/AVIF
- Use clean, keyword-relevant URLs
- Maintain canonical tags and social graph tags per page

## On-Page SEO Guidelines

- One clear H1 per page tied to intent
- Use H2 blocks for category context and product details
- Internal links across home -> category -> product -> related products
- Use descriptive image alt text with product name + variant

## Off-Page SEO Plan

- Build backlinks from Pakistani fashion blogs and digital magazines
- Run Instagram/TikTok short-form style content with product tagging
- Partner with Pakistani micro-influencers for conversion campaigns

## Local SEO Plan

- Emphasize location phrases: `online clothing store Pakistan`, `best fashion brand in Pakistan`
- Keep PKR pricing, local shipping details, and Pakistan trust signals visible
- Add location relevance in metadata and supporting copy

## Competitor Positioning (Khaadi, Gul Ahmed, Outfitters)

- Compete through minimal luxury differentiation, not catalog size
- Focus on long-tail transactional pages they under-optimize
- Win with cleaner UX, faster pages, and stronger product storytelling
